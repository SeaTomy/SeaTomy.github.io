Team Information

Get to know SeaTomy - our mission, expertise, and team members

Our Mission

SeaTomy is dedicated to advancing cybersecurity knowledge through competitive CTF challenges. We strive to push the boundaries of what's possible in ethical hacking and security research.

Our mission extends beyond winning competitions - we aim to contribute to the cybersecurity community through knowledge sharing and mentoring upcoming security professionals.

Core Expertise

  • Web Application Security
  • Binary Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Cryptography & Cryptanalysis
  • Digital Forensics
  • Network Security
  • Steganography
  • OSINT (Open Source Intelligence)

Competition Focus

We actively participate in various CTF competitions including:

  • DEF CON CTF
  • PlaidCTF
  • Google CTF
  • PicoCTF
  • HITCON CTF
  • Regional and local competitions

Training & Development

We believe in continuous learning and skill development:

  • Weekly team practice sessions
  • Vulnerability research projects
  • Security tool development
  • Conference presentations
  • Community workshops and talks
